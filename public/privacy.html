Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Overview

WhiteKim helps businesses with lead generation, outbound systems, and sales follow-up. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website, contact us, book a call, or communicate with us.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Name, email address, phone number, company name, and job title.
  • Messages, call notes, meeting details, and business needs you share with us.
  • Website usage information such as browser type, pages viewed, and device data.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Respond to questions, schedule calls, and provide requested services.
  • Send business communications related to WhiteKim services.
  • Improve our website, systems, offers, and customer experience.
  • Maintain security, prevent misuse, and comply with legal duties.

SMS and Text Messaging

If you provide your mobile number or ask us to follow up by phone or SMS, you agree that WhiteKim may send one-to-one business text messages related to your request, conversation, meeting, or interest in our services. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help.

We do not sell, rent, or share mobile opt-in information or SMS consent data with third parties for their marketing or promotional purposes. We may share information with service providers only when needed to deliver messages, operate our systems, or provide our services.

Sharing Information

We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate our website, communications, CRM, analytics, email, SMS, or business systems. We may also share information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, or during a business transfer.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. No online system is perfectly secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You can ask us to update or delete your contact information, subject to legal and business record requirements. You can opt out of SMS by replying STOP to any message.
